About This Website

This website is designed by Yinuo Chen, a cryptography enthusiast and developer. The purpose of this site is to provide interactive tools and resources for exploring classical cryptographic methods, including Shift Cipher, Permutation Cipher, and Substitution Cipher. These tools allow users to encrypt, decrypt, and perform cryptanalysis without a key, offering a hands-on approach to understanding these ancient techniques.

For those interested in deepening their knowledge of cryptography, the website recommends the book "Cryptography Theory and Practice" (2nd Edition, Classical Cryptography) as an excellent resource. Additionally, further insights and practical examples can be found at Practical Cryptography.
